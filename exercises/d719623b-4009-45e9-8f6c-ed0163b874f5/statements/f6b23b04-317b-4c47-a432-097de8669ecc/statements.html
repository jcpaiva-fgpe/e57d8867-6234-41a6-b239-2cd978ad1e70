Runner

Running at a slow pace takes you 8 minutes and 15 seconds per mile and running at a moderate pace takes you 7 minutes and 12 seconds per mile.
Write a time_arrival function (n, m) which, assuming a departure at 6.52 AM, given n miles traveled at a slow pace and m at a moderate pace, PRINTS the arrival time.

Input

The first line contains the n miles traveled at a slow pace.
The second line contains the m miles traveled at a moderate pace.

Output

The first line contains the arrival time.

Sample Input Sample Output
0
0		 6:52
2
5		 7:44
8
5		 9:34
