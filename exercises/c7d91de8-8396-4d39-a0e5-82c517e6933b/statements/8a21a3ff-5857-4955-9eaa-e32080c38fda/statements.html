Even or Odd

Your task is to write a short program that takes in input M numbers and returns two outputs: E the total of even numbers, U the total of uneven numbers

Input

The first line contains a number N that is the expected numbers to take in input.
The second line contains all the N numbers divided by space “ “ of the array A.

Output

The output contains E and U divided by space.

Sample Input Sample Output
3
23 4 56		 2 1
6
12 34 22 3 45 2		 4 2
5
2 4 6 8 10		 5 0
