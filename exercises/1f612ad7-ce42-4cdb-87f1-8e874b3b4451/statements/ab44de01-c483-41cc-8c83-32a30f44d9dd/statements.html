Prime numbers

Calculates the prime numbers between two integers A and B entered by the user

Input

The first line contains the two numbers

Output

The first line contains all the prime numbers separated by space “ “.

Sample Input Sample Output
44 93 47 53 59 61 67 71 73 79 83 89
23 45 23 29 31 37 41 43
3 9 3 5 7
